  • A virtual assistant is an artificial intelligence-powered software program designed to provide assistance to users in various tasks. It is essentially a digital assistant that uses natural language processing and machine learning algorithms to understand and respond to users' requests. Virtual assistants can be accessed through devices such as smartphones, smart speakers, and computers, and can perform a wide range of tasks, such as scheduling appointments, setting reminders, providing weather forecasts, and even ordering groceries. They are increasingly becoming a common feature in both personal and professional settings, allowing users to save time and increase productivity by delegating routine tasks to a digital assistant.
